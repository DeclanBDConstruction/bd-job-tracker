      My Diary

      A private journal, just for you — nobody else, not even admins, can see what you write here. Opens on today by default — use the arrows or the date picker above to look at another day. Tick something off once it's done and it turns green; ticked items clear off the diary at the end of the day, and anything left unticked rolls over to today so it doesn't get lost.

        Risk Assessments

        Generic templates, plus any you've uploaded or saved yourself — view, edit and attach straight to a job's RAMS documents. Editing a generic or saved risk assessment doesn't change the original: use Save As to keep your changes as a new one in this list. Always review and adjust for the actual site conditions before anyone works to one.
